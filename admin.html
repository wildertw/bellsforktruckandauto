How this admin works
  • Edits are saved to this browser (localStorage) and a fresh inventory.json is downloaded.
  • Upload the downloaded inventory.json to your website root.
  • Photos are renamed + downloaded (they do not auto-upload). Put photos into /assets/vehicles/.
