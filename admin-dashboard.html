How Leads Are Classified

Leads are estimated proportionally based on where site traffic originated during the same period. Since individual form/call events are not tagged per-page, classification uses page-view ratios as a proxy.

🔥
Hot Lead

Visitor was on a specific vehicle detail page (VDP) before contacting you — they already found a car they're interested in. Highest purchase intent. Capped at 60% of total leads.

▶ Follow up immediately — offer a test drive or price quote on that vehicle.
☀
Warm Lead

Visitor was browsing the inventory list page — actively shopping but hasn't picked a specific vehicle yet. Moderate purchase intent. Capped at 30% of total leads.

▶ Ask what they're looking for and suggest matching vehicles from your lot.
❄
Cold Lead

Visitor came from the homepage, contact page, blog, financing page, or another general page — early-stage curiosity, not yet vehicle-shopping. Remaining leads after hot & warm are removed.

▶ Nurture with inventory highlights, financing options, or a follow-up call to qualify interest.
